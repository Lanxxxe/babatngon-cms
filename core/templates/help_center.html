Help Center

Find answers and get assistance with the Babatngon CMS

Getting Started

Creating an Account

Register as a resident by clicking "Get Started" and filling out the registration form with your valid information.

Logging In

Use your registered email and password to sign in to your account.

First Steps

After logging in, complete your profile and familiarize yourself with the dashboard.

Filing Complaints

How to File a Complaint
  1. Navigate to "File Complaint" in the dashboard
  2. Fill in the complaint details (title, description, category)
  3. Add location information
  4. Attach supporting documents or photos if needed
  5. Submit your complaint
Emergency Complaints

For urgent situations requiring immediate attention, use the "Emergency Complaint" option for faster response.

Requesting Assistance

Types of Assistance
  • Medical assistance
  • Financial aid
  • Educational support
  • Disaster relief
  • Other community services
Processing Time

Assistance requests are reviewed by barangay officials. Processing time varies depending on the type and urgency of the request.

Tracking Status

Status Updates

Track your complaints and assistance requests through "My Complaints" and "My Assistance" sections.

Notifications

Receive real-time notifications about status changes and admin responses via SMS and in-app notifications.

Follow-ups

You can send follow-up messages to admins regarding your pending cases directly through the system.

Notifications

SMS Notifications

Receive SMS alerts when your complaints or assistance requests are updated by admins.

In-App Notifications

Check your notification center for detailed updates and messages from barangay staff.

Notification Settings

Ensure your contact number is up-to-date in your profile to receive SMS notifications.

Account Management

Profile Updates

Keep your profile information current to ensure proper communication and service delivery.

Password Security

Change your password regularly and use strong passwords to protect your account.

Account Verification

Verified accounts have full access to system features. Contact barangay officials for verification assistance.

Still Need Help?

Contact the barangay office for additional support and assistance.
