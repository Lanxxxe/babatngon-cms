Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

Introduction

The Barangay Babatngon Complaint Management System ("we," "our," or "CMS") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register and use our system, we collect:

  • Full name (first, middle, last name, suffix)
  • Email address
  • Contact number
  • Address and barangay information
  • Profile picture (optional)
Complaint and Assistance Information
  • Complaint details and descriptions
  • Location data (address, coordinates)
  • Supporting documents and photos
  • Status updates and communications
Technical Information
  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Access times and dates

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and manage complaints and assistance requests
  • Communicate with you about case updates
  • Send SMS notifications regarding your submissions
  • Improve our services and system functionality
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud
  • Generate anonymous statistics for barangay reporting
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing and Disclosure

With Barangay Officials

Your information is shared with authorized barangay administrators and staff members who need access to process your complaints and assistance requests.

With Third-Party Services

We use Semaphore SMS service for sending notifications. Only necessary contact information is shared with this service provider.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Password encryption using bcrypt
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Access controls for administrative users
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Activity logging for accountability

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct your information through your profile
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account (subject to legal requirements)
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for non-essential communications
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured format

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Necessary to provide services to you
  • Required for legal, regulatory, or reporting purposes
  • Needed to resolve disputes or enforce our agreements

Cookies and Tracking

Our system uses session cookies to maintain your login state and improve user experience. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for residents who can legally file complaints in their community. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending notifications for material changes
